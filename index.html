🛡️ Scam Detector
AI scans job postings and recruiter messages for red flags, fake listings, and phishing attempts
⚠️ Real threats targeting job seekers right now
Fake recruiters steal personal info (SSN, bank details) through fake onboarding. Phishing links harvest LinkedIn/email credentials. Some fake job postings are identity theft setups. Never share SSN, bank info, or ID documents before accepting a verified offer.
🔍 Analyze Job Posting or Recruiter Message
🛡️ Analysis Results
📋 Common Scam Red Flags — Know What to Watch For
🚨 IMMEDIATE RED FLAGS
• Asks for SSN before offer letter
• Requests bank info for "payroll setup"
• Offers to mail check before you start
• Job pays unusually high with no experience needed
• Interview is only via text/WhatsApp
• Asks you to buy gift cards or equipment
⚠️ INVESTIGATE FURTHER
• Recruiter uses Gmail/Yahoo (not company email)
• Company hard to find on LinkedIn
• Job description is vague or generic
• Asked to click an external link to apply
• Can't verify company on Glassdoor/BBB
• Salary seems too high for the role described
✅ LEGITIMACY SIGNALS
• Company has Glassdoor reviews + LinkedIn page
• Recruiter email matches company domain
• Video interview with verified company account
• Offer comes through official company email
• Background check through verified vendor
• Company is on LinkedIn with 50+ employees
🔒 PROTECT YOURSELF
• Never share SSN until offer is signed
• Verify recruiter on LinkedIn before engaging
• Google "[Company name] + scam" + current year
• Check BBB, Glassdoor, LinkedIn company page
• If unsure — ask to speak on video call
• Report confirmed scams to FTC.gov
